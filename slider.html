We render this service within Lagos State only. Our service is strictly one on one training on any of the programming languages. We render this service in two ways;

  • Online Training
  • Home / Office Training

    • We teach all levels of candidates ranging from primary school pupils, high school students, undergraduates, graduates, working class, within the age of 12 to 65 years old. We take you from scratch to the expert level. We operate on per hour charge. Our One hour training service attracts a total sum of One Thousand, Five Hundred Naira only (# 1500), excluding transport fare. The transport fare depends on the location of our aspiring candidate.
